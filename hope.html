ABOUT US

People who borrow money are expected to follow certain rules to ensure fairness and responsibility. First, they should clearly understand and agree to the terms of the loan, including the amount, interest rate, and repayment schedule. Borrowers must use the money for its intended purpose and make payments on time to avoid penalties or legal consequences. It is also important to communicate honestly with the lender if any financial difficulties arise. Respecting these guidelines helps maintain trust and promotes healthy financial relationships.
